Volante Teams



Training Team:

We focus heavily on fundamentals as we believe they should come before anything else. The training team sessions are four months long with three per year. As students progress there will be an opportunity to showcase their talents as part of a student performance team. We have a wide range of end goals for our students; whether it be becoming part of the Volante performance team, gaining confidence social dancing or just honing in on basics. Our training team’s mission is to give enough individual attention to accomplish all of these goals.

Student Performance Team:

Our student performance team is a good middle ground between the training team and our travel performance team. This team trains in technique for the first six months of the year, to then learn/perform a choreography for the second six months of the year. It is designed to be a stepping stone before the travel performance team. While we still focus on technique, we start to learn much more about the performance aspects, with smaller local performances to get us started!

Travel Performance Team:

The original Volante team consists of two different technique tracks that combine into one cohesive performance team! This is designed to place each dancer at their appropriate level. That way we can inspire each other — learning side-by-side — prior to sharing the stage together. This team requires the highest level of commitment. Our travel performance season lasts for one year from September to September. Six of those months we are performing in and out of the country!

Volante hopes to be a constant reminder of what empowered women artists look like.
